Frequently asked questions What movies are Now Showing at iNi cinemas? Visit the “Now Showing” section in our website iNi Cinemas Is it possible to buy movie tickets online? Of course, you can make Online Movie Tickets Purchase through our website iNi Cinemas Which Online payment options are accepted? You can buy tickets online using our payment partners; Khalti or eSewa. How can I confirm my Ticket Purchase? After the completion of the purchasing process, a clear confirmation of your purchase is displayed on your screen. Your e-tickets will appear in the "Purchased" section of your "My Tickets" page. Just present your e-tickets to enter the theater. What movies are Next Change/Coming Soon at iNi Cinemas? Click on the “Next Change”/"Coming Soon" section at our website. How can I find out if my reservation has been confirmed? After the completion of the reservation process, a clear confirmation of your reservation is displayed on your screen. Your reserved seats will appear in the "Booked" section of your "My Tickets" page. When can I collect the tickets I've reserved? Customers must collect their tickets at least 45 minutes before the start of the show because the reservation is automatically released at that time. Please be aware that reservations for morning shows are released 1 day prior at 7 PM. How can I contact iNi Cinemas? You can reach us at +977-9802317400 or +977-9802317444 or mail us at marketing@inicinemas.com for all your queries and suggestions. Do I have to pay for children? Children above 3 ft. require a separate ticket. Can we take outside food into the movie hall? Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the theater (including chewing gum, gutkha, cigarettes etc.) Are there any additional charges for booking or purchasing tickets online? Online reservations and ticket purchases are free of additional charges. What are the parking facilities, and do I have to pay for parking at iNi cinemas? We have our own parking facility at iNi Cinemas. Parking charges will be as per the mall’s policy. Can I get a refund or reschedule the ticket I purchased? Movie Tickets & Food once purchased cannot be exchanged, rescheduled, cancelled or refunded. What do I do if customer care doesn't pick up my calls? When there is a lot of demand for the movies, we get a lot of calls, due to which the incoming calls are queued or missed sometime. It is not that we do not receive your calls; it is just that all our customer care representatives are busy attending another call. For normal inquiries you can check our website or you can message us on our social media handles (Facebook or Instagram). *Social Media links are mentioned at the bottom of our website* How do I make a hall reservation?
